About Us

Retrospect Apparel Co. was founded during the pandemic by a couple of friends with a realization that most people like to rekindle their
past memories especially in this trying times. We started this brand to share different experiences, and perspectives using the shirts we
produce as a canvas. We aim to create something that exceeds our existence.


It is our goal to create a community where people has the same interests and a platform for artist to express and introduce their art
and stories. This brand is the embodiment of our vision for the future of the youth, we aspire to bring them together and make movements
towards the goal we believe in together. Together, Let's do this one byte at a time.
